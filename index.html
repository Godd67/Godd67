Overview

Awesome! Rarely available spacious 4 bedrooms/3 bathrooms end unit in highly desirable Southwest Village condominium association in Sampleton.

The main level features hardwood flooring, large bright living room with a gas fireplace and a french door to the backyard facing wooden deck, open style kitchen with a double sink, garbage disposer and plenty of storage, spacious dining room with vaulted ceilings and a bedroom/office room with dedicated full bathroom, tile flooring and vaulted ceilings.

Brightly lit wooden stairwell goes upstairs, where a master suit with a master bathroom and a walk-in closet with california style shelving is situated. There are also two other bedrooms and another full bathroom upstairs.

Hardwood floors on the main level, new carpet in upstairs bedrooms. The unit also has a spacious unfinished basement with walk out, natural gas heating, full house air humidifier, central air conditioner and central vacuum. Very convenient location in Sampleton, short drive to highway and Bristol shopping plazas, low condo fees. Complex is tree lined with walking trails for exercising and dog walking.
